# Draft: Remote Work and Data Handling Policy

**Status:** DRAFT – HR & Legal Review  
**Effective date (proposed):** TBD

---

## 1. Scope

This policy applies to all employees and contractors who access company systems or handle company data from non-office locations.

## 2. Data Handling

- **Confidential and strictly confidential** documents must not be stored on personal devices. Use only approved company endpoints and cloud drives.
- **Personal data** of employees (e.g., performance reviews, salary data, health-related information) must be accessed only on a need-to-know basis and must not be emailed to personal accounts or printed for home use unless explicitly permitted by HR.
- When in doubt, treat information as confidential and restrict distribution.

## 3. Device and Network

- Company-issued devices must be used for all work involving customer data, financial data, or unreleased product information.
- Public Wi‑Fi may be used only with an active VPN connection to the corporate network.
- Screen sharing and recording in third-party tools (e.g., Zoom, Teams) must follow the “no sensitive data on screen” guideline for external calls.

## 4. Breach and Incidents

- Suspected data loss or unauthorized access must be reported to Security and HR within 24 hours.
- Failure to comply may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.
