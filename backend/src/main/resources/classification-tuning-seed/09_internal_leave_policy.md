# Annual Leave and Absence Policy

**Effective:** January 1, 2025  
**Applies to:** All full-time employees

---

## Entitlement

- Full-time employees receive 18 days of annual leave per calendar year, pro-rated for joiners and leavers.
- Leave must be requested through the HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance for periods of 5 or more consecutive days.
- Unused leave may be carried over up to 5 days into the next year if approved by your manager.

## Sick Leave

- Up to 12 days of paid sick leave per year. Medical certificate required for absences of 3 or more consecutive days.
- For extended illness, refer to the separate Long-Term Absence policy.

## Public Holidays

- The company observes the local public holidays of your primary work location. A list is published on the intranet each year.
