# Payroll Processing – Strictly Confidential

**Restricted to:** HR and Finance Payroll team only  
**Document ID:** HR-PAY-2025-01

---

## Data Handling

- Payroll data (employee IDs, bank account details, salary, tax identifiers, bonuses) must be processed only on designated secure systems.
- No payroll files may be sent to personal email or stored on unencrypted removable media.
- Access is logged and audited quarterly.

## Bank Details and Tax IDs

- Employee bank account numbers (full account number and routing details) are stored in the payroll system with encryption at rest.
- Tax identification numbers (e.g. SSN, National ID where applicable) are used solely for statutory reporting and must not be included in any export to non-HR systems.
- Any change to bank or tax data requires dual approval and is logged.

## Distribution

- Payslips are delivered via the secure employee self-service portal only. Paper payslips are not issued.
- Managers must not request or retain copies of team members’ payslips or salary data except as required for approved compensation reviews (see Compensation Policy).

---

**Classification: STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL. Unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse may result in disciplinary action and legal liability.**
