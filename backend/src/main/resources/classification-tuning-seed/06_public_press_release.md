# Press Release: Company Opens New Regional Office

**For immediate release**  
**Date:** March 1, 2025

---

We are pleased to announce the opening of our new regional office in Singapore. The expansion supports our growing presence in Asia-Pacific and will create approximately 80 new jobs over the next 18 months.

The new facility includes modern workspace, a customer briefing center, and training rooms. It will serve as the hub for our sales and support teams in the region.

"We are committed to investing in the communities where we operate," said the CEO. "This opening reflects our confidence in the regional market and our long-term growth strategy."
